Final Project Process

UI/UX User Testing

My Every Picture studio project is currently a page that displays one of my many interests, growing my plants. For my Final Project I will be conducting user test in order to understand how to better the user experience of my Every Picture Project.

Blow you can check the feedback I got from user testing by clicking on the name of the user.

1. Demographic Information:
What is your age?
What is your occupation?
2. Pre-Test Questions:
Just glancing at the page, what are your expectations for what the page is for?
3. During Task Execution:
Can you walk me through what you are doing and why?
How do you feel about the layout/design of this page/screen?
Are there any elements that confuse you or that you find difficult to understand?
Did you encounter any issues while completing this task?
4. Post-Task Questions:
Were there any features or functionalities missing that you expected to find?
What aspects do you like the most?
5. Overall Impressions:
Do you have any additional comments or feedback?
